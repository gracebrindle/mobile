Library Room Reservation System


Client: Big Rapids Community Library
Need: To update their room reservation system for their post-pandemic reopening
Timeline: September to December 2021

As part of a course on contextual inquiry and consulting, I am working with a small public library to improve their room reservation system. I completed a background research report and worked with a team of four peers to create an interview protocol. My team completed six interviews with various library staff members and recorded affinity notes. We then constructed an affinity wall of over 150 notes to analyze our data and draw conclusions.
